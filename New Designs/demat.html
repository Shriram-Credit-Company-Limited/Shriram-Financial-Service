What Is A Demat Account?

An electronic account that holds your financial securities — stocks, bonds, ETFs, mutual fund units, and government securities — in digital form instead of physical certificates. It is maintained by CDSL or NSDL through a SEBI-registered Depository Participant like Shriram Financial Services. Combined with a Trading account (opened simultaneously), it gives you the ability to buy and sell securities on NSE and BSE instantly.

Held With

Your securities sit with CDSL or NSDL — SEBI-regulated, government-backed central depositories — not on paper.

What You Can Hold

Equity Shares, Bonds, ETFs, Mutual Fund units and Government Securities — together in a single account.

Am I Ready To Open A Demat Account?

If you can tick all five boxes below, you are ready to open your account right now.

  • ✓You are 18 years of age or older (minors can invest through a registered guardian)
  • ✓You have a valid PAN card in your name
  • ✓You have an Aadhaar card linked to your active mobile number
  • ✓You have an active bank account in your name — savings or current
  • ✓You are an Indian resident (NRIs can invest via RBI-approved NRE/NRO routes)
